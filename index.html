Homepage of Anders Dalskov

Hi! I got my PhD from Aarhus University in 2020, where I spent a good three years in the Crypto Group under supervision of Claudio Orlandi, working primarily with concretely efficient secure multiparty computation, or MPC, as it's also often called.

At the moment I'm doing whatever a cryptographic engineer is supposed to be doing at Partisia, a company which solves problems with and related to MPC and Blockchains.

Picture is me. I'm a lot nicer than it lets on :)

This page mostly serves as a place for me to put random thoughts and to collect information about my publications, talks, and so on. Other than that this page is a pretty run-of-the-mill static personal webpage.
